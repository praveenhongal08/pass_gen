About

Dr.Suresh Kammar

Dr. Suresh Kammar was born in 1969 in the Karnataka state. After having received graduation in Drawing, he has rendered his services as a Pioneer Teacher. In the filed of Kannada Film Industry, Professional Art field and even worked as a Musician, Singer, Director, Judge and guide for the last 25 years.

Later, after being given such a great service, now that is oflate, recently just before 7 years he alone has established Life Touch Foundation which is social service center and even worked towards its upliftment. Along with this he has indulged himself in the social service for welfare of the rural youth and their progress in the education as well as their development. Keeping in the mind, such a large progress, even he has implemented some of the new programmes and free computer education camps; so as to contribute for the development of the youth belonging to slum areas and for the women living in the backward areas, he has conducted so many free self employment programmes.


Awareness and consciousness programmes, and given good guidance, encouragement. Further, he has planned some of the Industrial orientation, training camps, especially for the rural and urban backward territories. Later, through these channels, he has writed women across these areas and constructed ‘Self – help – groups’

Moreover, in most of the occasions, he has given financial assistance to make them self – reliant and self – dependent. Further, he worked as a Human - Right - Activist as well as, member of the state. Obliviously he has helped many in eradicating Injustice – Tortures - Encroachment - Ill treatment. So by the virtue of which, he stood as a mighty power against the violation of Human Rights and he has taken some special care; by surveying some of the local thoughts. Even, he had a discussion with the concerned officials to help guide and restrain the particular and justice and social development and for the public interest as well as state interest and stood distinctly for his unselfished service.

Owing to such volunteer social - service, he has become the recipient of ‘National Social Gold Award’, ‘Best Social Worker 2010’ Award. Like this, he not only received , so many awards, but  also bagged ‘Honorable Doctorate Award in Social Service’
